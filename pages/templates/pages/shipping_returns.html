Delivery and Returns Policy

Delivery

All items are sent by Royal Mail "Tracked 48 with Signature" tracked. If you have choosen an order from the online shop, your order will be despatched by the next working day. Once the order is despatched, you will receive an email with a Royal Mail Tracking number. Please note that if you are ordering from abroad, your purchase may incur customs charges. You will need to cover these costs yourself.

Returns Policy

We hope you will you love your artwork, however, we realize there is a risk to damage during delivery or the item might not be quite what you expected.
  • 28 days return period
  • The artwork must be in the same condition you received it.
  • Paper Dreams eserves the right to refuse the return or deduct from a refund any diminution of value as a result of the product not being in the above condition.
  • Return via Post Office or courier of your choice, we can not cover delivery charges for returns with the exception of items damaged during delivery.
  • Refunds can take up to 10 working days.
  • You will be refunded to your original method of payment.
  • Once we have processed your return/exchange you will receive a confirmation email.
  • Please allow 3-5 working days from receipt of email for the refund to show on your statement.
