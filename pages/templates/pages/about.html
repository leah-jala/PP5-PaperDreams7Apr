About Paper Dreams

When my daughters were 2, I made a papier mache pumpkin to the Halloween candy we'd hand out. I didn't expect much, but when it was done I was wowed by it. I started Googling for ideas for papier mache, and I was suprised to discover that the famous Commedia dell'arte masks were made in this way. This sent me down a path of discovery. I taught myself the basic skills, and eventually went to Florence to work with a master mask-maker. In between, I attended some courses at local universities and adult colleges to hone skills in drawing, sculpture, painting and mould-making. After a lot of encouragement I started selling my masks and other objects and the rest is history! I hope my site will inspire you to give a try. Keep an eye on my blog for upcoming tutorials.
