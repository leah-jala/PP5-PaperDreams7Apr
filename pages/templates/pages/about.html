About Paper Dreams

It started with a pumpkin!

When my daughters were 2, I made a papier mache pumpkin for Halloween candy. I was wowed by it, and again with the medium when I learned that the famous Commedia dell'arte masks were made in this way. This sent me down a path of discovery. I taught myself the basic skills, and eventually went to Florence to work with a master mask-maker. After a lot of encouragement I started selling my masks and other objects and the rest is history! I hope my site will inspire you to give a try.
